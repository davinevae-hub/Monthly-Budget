Your New Budgeting Calculator

Welcome! This calculator helps you estimate your biweekly take-home pay and organize your expenses. It uses a simple tax percentage deduction (default 22%) and does not include insurance, retirement, or other benefits. The goal: give you a clear picture of how many hours you need to work to cover your bills.

How to Use:

  1. Enter your hourly pay and hours worked per week.
  2. Add your tax %, or leave blank to use the default 22%.
  3. Click “Calculate Net Pay” to see your biweekly income.
  4. Fill in your expenses (Rent, Utilities, Other are already listed — add more as needed).
  5. Click “Calculate Budget” to view totals and hours needed to cover bills.
  6. Download results as PDF or CSV, or reset to start over.

Income Details

If unsure, leave blank — the calculator will use 22%. This is a simple estimate and does not include benefits (like insurance or retirement).
